About Woda

Woda is sourced from pristine springs deep in the Polish mountains. Every drop travels through natural rock formations, gaining a clean, refreshing mineral profile before it reaches your bottle.

Why Woda?

We believe water should taste exactly how nature made it—nothing added, nothing taken away. Woda is bottled at the source to preserve its purity, taste, and natural mineral balance.

  • Naturally filtered through ancient rock layers
  • Clean, mineral-rich taste
  • Perfect for daily hydration
  • Bottled at the source in the Polish mountains

Sustainability

Pure water deserves a pure planet. We’re committed to protecting both our springs and the environment around them.

Recyclable Packaging

Our bottles are 100% biodegradable, designed to be reused, recycled, and kept out of landfill. This kind of bottle can be turned back to soil, making it 100% from nature.

Responsible Sourcing

Eco-conscious Production

Our Source

Straight from the heart of the Polish mountains, Woda flows through untouched natural landscapes before reaching your bottle.

From spring to bottle

Woda begins its journey as rainfall and snowmelt, slowly filtered through layers of rock and mineral-rich earth. By the time it reaches our springs, it’s naturally balanced, clean, and ready to be bottled.

No additives. No artificial processing.

We don’t alter what nature has perfected. Woda is simply collected, gently filtered for clarity, and bottled—so what you drink is as close to the source as possible.
